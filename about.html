About Chanthiran's App

Welcome to my personal productivity and tracking hub! Chanthiran's App is designed to bring together various personal tools and utilities into one convenient, easy-to-access location.

The goal of this application is to streamline your daily tracking and management needs, from personal finance to fitness goals and travel planning, all within a simple and intuitive interface.

Mission

To empower individuals by providing a consolidated and user-friendly platform for managing personal data, achieving goals, and organizing daily life more efficiently.

Key Features

  • Run Tracker: Monitor your running distances and track progress towards your fitness targets.
  • Utilities: A collection of handy mini-tools for everyday tasks.
  • Travel Checklist: Ensure you're prepared for every journey with comprehensive packing lists.
  • Debtors: Keep track of personal loans and repayments.
  • Trip Expenses: Easily log and analyze your spending during trips.
  • Currency Converter: Quickly convert between different currencies for travel or financial planning.
  • Monthly Expenses Tracker: Monitor and categorize your monthly spending to better manage your budget.
  • Finance Tracker: Get a comprehensive overview of your financial inflows and outflows.
  • Rental Tracker: Keep track of rental payments, due dates, and property-related expenses.
